PINC Summer Program Research students Aline Moran, Emilio Lazcano, Evilio Banegas, and their mentor Spencer Kirkman presented their summer project 'Improving Lithium-Ion Batteries Using Machine Learning' at the 32nd Annual SFSU Summer Undergraduate Rsearch Symposium on July 26, 2025. They did great work this summer learning Python and applying their skills to modeling amorphous lithium fluoride.
